— zines —

little worlds
in your pocket.

Handmade. Heartfelt. Typewritten & collaged & yours.

Over the past few years, I've written, designed, and published four zines that explore everything from radical love to slowing down, forgiveness, and becoming. Each one is a little world of its own, filled with typewritten poems, collaged textures, personal reflections, and the soft mess of being human.

I've had the joy of vending at Milwaukee Zine Fest for four years, sharing my work with a community I deeply love. Whether you're here because we met at a fest, you stumbled across my work online, or you're just curious: welcome.

Available for purchase. Open to trades & sliding scale pricing. Reach out to arrange.
