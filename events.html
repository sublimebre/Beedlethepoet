frequently asked questions

What do you do at events?

I write original poems for guests using a prompt or theme. People stop by, give me a word, a feeling, or a story, and I turn it into a one-of-a-kind poem in just a few minutes.

How long does it take per poem?

About 5–7 minutes, depending on the complexity of the prompt and the vibe of the event. I can sling them out shorter and quicker, if we decide that's the direction!

Do you bring everything you need?

Yes! I bring my typewriter, a table, setup decor, and all materials. I just need a small space and access to shade/shelter if the event is outside.

Can I request a theme or custom prompt for my event?

Absolutely. I love curating the experience around your event — whether it's love poems for a wedding, gratitude notes for a nonprofit gala, or haikus for a campus party.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on the event type, duration, and travel. I'm happy to work with budgets and offer sliding scale options for grassroots and community-centered events. Just ask!

If you're planning an event and want something heartfelt, handmade, and a little bit different — reach out. Based in Milwaukee, available for travel throughout the Midwest (and beyond, with some planning!).
