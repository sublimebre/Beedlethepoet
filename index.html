on-demand poetry

you might be wondering what I do
with my typewriter...

  1. 01. We have a conversation. It can be short. Or maybe it takes a few minutes. You tell me what's on your heart and mind.
  2. 02. We land on your poem topic. You might know what that topic is before we even speak. What's important is that you feel it.
  3. 03. I type your poem in real time on my typewriter.
  4. 04. I read your poem to you.
  5. 05. Maybe we both cry — it happens! What I can guarantee is that you walk away with a one-of-a-kind poem.

Does that sound like something your soul wants? Let's get in touch.

The world is a beautiful place; thanks for taking time to experience it with me.
