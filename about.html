Howdy.

I'm Bre Legan (they/them), also known as Beedle. the Poet.

the person behind the typewriter

I'm a poet, zinester, lyricist, and typewriter enthusiast based in the Midwest. I'm pleased to say that my first poem was published in third grade, and I haven't stopped writing since.

I create work that's rooted in presence, connection, and care — poetry as a practice of noticing and remembering. Whether I'm typing spontaneous poems at events, vending handmade zines at festivals, or crafting lyrics with my band, my creative work explores themes of love, grief, identity, place, and becoming.

Much of my work lives in analog spaces: one-of-a-kind poems typed on my vintage typewriters, multimedia zines filled with reflection and collage, and commissioned poems that meet people right where they are.

beyond beedle

public poetry

Spray Painted Poems

I'm the creator of Spray Painted Poems, a public poetry project that brings short-form poems to everyday sidewalks. The project began during my time as Woodland Pattern Book Center's inaugural Milwaukee Emerging Poet Fellow (2022–23) and continues through participatory events and installations across Wisconsin.

music

The Furies

I'm a lyricist and guitarist for The Furies, an indie band exploring sound as another form of storytelling.

academia
