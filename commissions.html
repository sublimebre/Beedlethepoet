— commissions —

a poem
made for you.

The words your heart already knows but hasn't found yet.

the process

  1. 01. Reach out and tell me a little about what you're looking for. There's no wrong way to start.
  2. 02. We'll have a conversation — by email, or voice if you prefer. You tell me what's on your heart.
  3. 03. I type your poem on my typewriter. You receive a scan of the original typed page, plus the physical poem mailed to you if you'd like.
  4. 04. You carry those words with you.

what people commission

Poems have been commissioned for —

  • Weddings & anniversaries
  • Grief & loss
  • Birthdays & milestones
  • Transitions: new chapters, endings, beginnings
  • Gifts for people you love and can't find the words for
  • Just because the moment asked for it

what it costs

Commissions are priced on a sliding scale based on length and complexity. I never want cost to be a barrier — please reach out even if you're not sure what you can afford. We'll figure it out.

Community-centered and grassroots requests are welcome. If you want to gift a poem but can't pay full price, ask.

Ready to start? Send me a message. No pressure, no forms. Just words.
